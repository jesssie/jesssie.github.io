Green, Ho, The Rushes Grow

I'll sing you one, ho
Green, ho, the rushes grow
What is your one, ho?
One is one and all alone
And evermore shall be so.

I'll sing you two, ho
Green, ho, the rushes grow
What is your two, ho?
Two, two, the lily-white boys,
Clothed all in green, HO! HO!
One is one and all alone
And evermore shall be so.

And evermore shall be so.
